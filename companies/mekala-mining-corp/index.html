Your Protection

Mekala Mining Corporation is the armor of the Conglomerate. We provide Vanguard armor in five variants, meeting the needs of any mission.

Frontal Assault

Outfit your Vanguard with our assault armor for rapid attack operations. Conglomerate expeditionary forces have been relying on Mekala's vehicle assault armor since the war on Auraxis started. Through meticulous testing and constant refinement, Mekala has developed assault armor that increases the Vanguard's staying power in a head-on battle by 30%.

Side Standard

Fighting on the snowy plains of Esamir can mean leaving your Vanguard open to ambush and side attacks. We developed side plating at the request of the NC's Mechanized Operations division. When dancing with more agile foes, such as the Vanu Magrider, side armor plating can help defend you against being outmanuevered.

Back Protection

Preparing to wade through the swamps of Hossin? Heavy foliage on any battlefield can mean ambush attacks from all angles. Our back armor plating keeps you safe, by buying you the precious moments you need to locate the direction of an attacker and then end the threat.

Top Cover

In tests against Terran Mosquito aircraft, our top cover armor makes the Vanguard virtually impervious to machine gun fire and highly resistant to Breaker rocketpods. Fighting the air from the ground means making a commitment to superior staying power. Top cover armor gives your Vanguard the protection it needs from above, so you can stay the course and win the day.

Running Solid

12% of Vanguard tanks fall to enemy tank mines. This cowardly practice of deploying mines has become commonplace for Terran and Vanu combat engineers. Protect yourself and your squad by investing in our underarmor plating that will defend your vehicle from embedded explosives and mines.
